Work Summary

CURRENT PROJECT

Rural areas provide a sense of quiet and calm solitude, natural beauty, extended personal space, and care and warmth offerend in the close-knit communities. A recent trend in Californian lifestyle is to find affordable housing not too far from the San Francisco Bay Area where people can travel to and work when required. This rural-urban work life symbiosis comes with certain limitations like poor connectivity in rural areas. My current project explores some of these shortcomings.

Published Projects

Discovering Dark Patterns on Streaming Platforms

Published in DIS 2022     [pdf]

  • Companies promote binge watching on streaming platforms.
  • We did a diary study across 228 viewing sessions to gain insights into user moods while they watch videos.
  • We then list problematic user watching trends, and recognize dark (deceptive) UI patterns.
  • Our study highlights 4 ethical UI design considerations for sustainable and healthy viewing user habits.

  • Learn more

Designing a Speech Improvement application

Published in Mobile HCI 2021     [pdf]

  • People with English as second language have difficulty in speech communication.
  • We discover 8 design requirements necessary for a speech improvement application using --- cue-card study, low-fidelity wireframes, and audio-visual surveys.
  • We then test the final high-fidelity mobile application on 15 users for user insights on app experience.
  • The study provides proof of the effectiveness of our app design in helping users improve their speech expressions.

Class Projects

Designing for Emotion Regulation in Dogs

  • Dogs feel separation anxiety when isolated from owners.
  • I conducted 3 interviews with dog owners for exploring their interaction patterns with their dogs.
  • We came up with 4 non-anthropomorphic design solutions for an embodied tool that can help regulate dog emotions when not with their owners.
  • Our interviews provide initial proof for 4 design classifications that help explore the design space of an embodied tool which can be utilized for designing dog toys.

Data Sonification

  • Artists face a problem in having a common cross-modal media transfer unit that can help communicate and edit MIDI messages across different softwares.
  • We designed a python-based toolbox with a Kivy GUI for converting, editing and navigating MIDI messages across different softwares, like Reaper and SuperCollider.
  • Our tool has implications in acting as a bridging backbone for creative work across different platforms.
