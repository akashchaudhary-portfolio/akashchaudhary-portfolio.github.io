First employee, Derbii

Derbii is a monthly door to door cab pooling service for regular office commuters commuting at fixed times. The cab picks you up from right outside your home and drops you right at your office doorsteps. A fixed driver and a fixed cab are allotted to a fixed group of passengers commuting on a particular route on a daily basis. The service costs half the price as charged by Uber with a reduced hassle of not getting to book a cab every day.

I worked as the first employee at the company and was primarily responsible for handling investor relations. I brought 3 prominent angel investors on the table for a pre-series A fund. Additionally, I was responsible for ensuring quality services to customers along with documenting their feedbacks on the possible improvements. Further, I was involved in discussions regarding the business strategies like managing product pricing, exploiting areas of organic growth and managing below the line marketing.
